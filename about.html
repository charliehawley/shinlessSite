About me

I am a video editor, projectionist, web developer and artist based in St Leonards-on-sea, East Sussex.

After graduating from Falmouth University in 2014, I moved to Peckham, London and began making music videos for local artists and friends. I unearthed a deep connection to all things visually ambiguous and adopted the mantra 'making things that aren't from things that are.'

Shortly thereafter, I moved to St Leonards-on-sea and - having explored my own limits and capabilities within an abundance of NLEs - I discovered more abstract methodologies of creating moving image.

Guiding hands and local artists Jude Montague and Becca Rose showed me programs like Hydra and TouchDesigner and my world was expanded. Like an ecstatic child with new toys, I'm now gleefully sampling everything there is to offer within these new fields.
