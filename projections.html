For the last two years I've been developing my practice in Touch Designer, creating audio reactive stage visuals using many sources, including: LIDAR cameras, video game capture, youtube videos, archive footage and digitised VHS tapes.

With experience comes an awareness of preference and I've managed to squash some bad habits.

Rather than piling new features onto already tangled patches, I've developed a template patch for duel video channels that has roots.

Commonly used features like Midi Input Selectors and Feedback Loops are nested inside Base Elements, making the patch less verbose and crowded with functions neatly contained.

You can download my template here.
